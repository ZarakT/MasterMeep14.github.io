The Plastic Project Hackathon

A perfect hackathon for students of all levels of experience

Rules

Your project will be given a 1-10 score by a panel of judges, once submitted to us which will indicate your placement. However, you can gain extra points for the following (note that the score you get is proportional to your score, meaning a 5 from one judge nets you 5 points).

  1. It’s a sellable product to a target demographic (3 points)

  2. It’s a fully remote application that can be continually used after the hackathon is over (5 points)

  3. It’s a physical device that can be continually used after the hackathon is over (3 points)

  4. It’s related to an issue in your actual community (3 points)

General Info about our hackathon

The hackathon will be fully remote, taking place from April 27th-29th starting and ending at 6pm PST on each day. Here are some basic things to know about our hackathon and how it’ll work.

  • At 6pm PST, the theme and prompt of our hackathon will be announced on our discord server, linked above.

  • From there, participants will have 72 hours to produce whatever they desire related to the prompt

  • You can work in teams of up to 4 for the project

  • Not all projects have to be coded or even technologically based! We are looking forward to projects of all formats (Though tech is encouraged and rewareded).

Criteria

The criteria us judges will use when grading your project are:

  1. Creativity and innovation

  2. Your presentation

  3. How you overcame challenges

    4. Things we hope you also keep in mind:

  4. Sustainability

  5. Scalability

  6. Use of tech (digital or physical)
